About Us

After years of living in fear, uncertainty, and doubt in the Security industry,

Our Story

Risk Log Book was founded on the idea: there is a better way to measure uncertainty to reduce risk. In a world of media hype and moving threat landscapes, it is critical that business objectives drive mitigations stratiges and corporations understand risk tolerance before they are held accountable for it.


We simply want to fix a broken industry to preserve value of the people that work in it.
